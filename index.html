Michoud Re-opens to Designated Employees

Acting NASA Administrator Robert Lightfoot viewed tornado repairs made after tornado damage to Michoud Assembly Facility Building 103 where NASA is making parts for the Space Launch System and Orion. Power has been restored to 75 percent of the 43-acre rocket factory. All SLS employees were back at work on Feb. 13, 2017, manufacturing part of the heavy lift rocket and assessing tooling and hardware.

Comet 2016 WF9

On Feb. 25, 2017 Comet 2016 WF9 will approach Earth's orbit. At a distance of nearly 32 million miles (51 million kilometers) from Earth, this pass will not bring it particularly close. The trajectory of 2016 WF9 is well understood, and is not a threat to Earth.
